How To Navigate The Timeline

1. The Marvel Comics Timeline is scrollable from left-to-right.

2. You can select the "Start Exploring" button, the "Filter" button, or the "Help" button.

3. The "Start Exploring" button will choose a random comic book story for you to view.

4. The "Filter" button allows you to filter the visible comic book stories to match selected character or event type tags.

5. After clicking on "Apply Filters" in the "Filter" pop-up window, a new button will appear to the right of the "Filters" button named "Remove Filters". Click on this to return the timeline back to normal.

6. The "Help" button displays this current navigation help pop-up window.

7. You may select a comic book story node directly on the timeline itself if you see one that interests you.

8. In any pop-up window, you may click on the "x" in the top-right corner of the window to close the window.
